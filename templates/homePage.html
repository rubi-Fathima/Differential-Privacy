What is Differential Privacy?

As the world progresses, we see huge amounts of data being collected by companies. They use this to improve upon their prodicts and services, and might not directly have any malicious purpose behind collecting user data. But this can not be promised once the companies get this data, as they have no accountability directly with the users.

But there is a way around this issue and benifit both parties, the user and the companies. This is where the concept of Differential Privacy steps in. The concept is to special libraries that manipulate a small sample of the data, or slightly change it's distribution. This adds an uncertainity as to the authenticity of individual datatapoints, while keeping the overall data distribution as close as possible.
